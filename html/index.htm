WorkFrame is a non-profit organization that helps you learn and understand technology.





We start clubs in different institutions where we try and make it easy to approach and learn technology. Experienced volunteers can take control of their local WorkFrame club and shape it according to their members' needs.


Learn

We help you learn things you can use in the real world today -- to find a job, explore your hobbies, or just do something you love.

Events

We regularly host events like hackathons, cross-college meetups, demos, workshops and seminars. You're welcome to come speak about something you love, teach something new, or just show us what you've been working on.

Community

Need help? Looking for someone to work on a project with you? Or do you just want to meet some like minded people to chat with? Our community is open to everybody.


If WorkFrame isn't in your institute, but you like what we're doing, consider starting a club yourself. We'll help you out any way we can, so don't hesitate.
