WorkFrame is a non-profit organization centered around making it easier to learn and understand technology.





Our initiative operates by having clubs across institutions where members are given easy access to approaching technology. Volunteers who are experienced can take control of their local WorkFrame club and push it forward by shaping it to their members' needs.


Learn

Our recommanded syllabus is focussed on practicality and real world usage of technology in contrast to standard college courses. Our volunteers will host sessions from time to time to cover these topics in your club.

Events

We use our combined audience to periodically host offline events such as hackathons, code challenges, etc. We also host workshops or seminars conducted by reputed keynote speakers when the demand arises.

Community

If you don't know why your program doesn't compile, or you need to find interested people for a project, or even to simply host a LAN party in your off hours, the WorkFrame community will help you out.


If WorkFrame doesn't exist in your college, but you like what we're doing, consider starting a club yourself.
